About Me

Borcsa Vass is a cinematic storyteller, weaving suspense and intricate twists and turns into scripts that challenge the boundaries of contemporary crime thrillers and dramas. She has been commissioned by production companies to develop original screenplays, and her work has received a Special Mention at the Asia Pacific Screen Awards.

Dedicated to a career in screenwriting and performance, Borcsa brings a distinctive, professional methodology to the artistic process. Her narratives are characterised by high-stakes tension and character-driven emotion, reflecting a profound understanding of the human psyche and genre mechanics. With a background as a barrister and lawyer, she brings a unique creative and commercial worldview to her projects.

Borcsa crafts immersive worlds that captivate both audience and industry professionals. Combining lived experience with a sharp cinematic eye, Borcsa continues to define new standards in professional storytelling.

She is collaborative and commercially-minded, and enjoys working alongside producers and filmmakers to bring their vision to life.
